Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sarveshchhetri/freelancer-success-earnings-and-churn-prediction

This dataset contains 12,000 realistic freelancer records from the 2025 gig economy. It is designed to closely mirror real data from platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and Freelancer.com.

The data includes detailed information about freelancer profiles, skills, performance metrics, earnings, and behavioral patterns. It is perfect for building predictive models to understand what drives success and retention in the freelance world.
